Privacy Policy for Pin Manager

Last Updated: 29/12/2023

Thank you for using Pin Manager (the "App"). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect:

a. User-Provided Information:

When you create an account or use the App, you may provide us with information such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
b. Pin Information:

The App allows users to upload and sort pins by date and other attributes. This pin information may include images, videos, and associated metadata.
c. Usage Data:

We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use, the content you view, and your interactions with other users.
d. Device Information:

We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, including the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
How We Use Your Information:

a. Pin Sorting and Searching:

Your pin information, including date and other attributes, is used to provide you with an easier way to sort and search through your uploaded content.
b. User Account Management:

Your user-provided information is used for account creation, authentication, and account management purposes.
c. App Improvement:

We use usage data and feedback to improve the App's functionality, user experience, and features.
d. Communications:

We may use your email address to send you important updates, notifications, and information related to the App.
Data Security:

a. We prioritize the security of your information and take reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

b. While we implement security measures, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services:

a. The App may integrate with third-party services for image editing or other features. Please review the privacy policies of these third-party services, as they may have different practices.

Data Sharing:

a. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

b. We may share aggregated and anonymized data for statistical and analytical purposes.

Children's Privacy:

a. The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

a. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated.

Contact Us:

a. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [contact@unbound.rip].

By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
